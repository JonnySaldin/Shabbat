About Us

During Oscar and Karla's 16 years of ministry, along with the Impacting Guatemala Network (IGN) team, God has allowed them to serve more than 140 churches in Guatemala and several countries in Latin America. Along this journey, they have discovered a profound truth: pastors—who pour themselves out daily for their congregations—also need rest, renewal, and ongoing training. Durante 16 años de ministerio de Oscar y Karla y del equipo de la Red Impactando a Guatemala -IGN-, Dios les ha permitido servir a más de 140 iglesias en Guatemala y en varios países de Latinoamérica. En este caminar han descubierto algo profundo: los pastores, que día a día entregan todo por sus congregaciones, también necesitan descanso, renovación y capacitación continua.

Pastors often carry overwhelming responsibilities: caring for the needs of their members, leading ministries, directing meetings, preparing sermons, and much more. In the midst of all this, they have no time to care for themselves, their spouses, and their children. The price is heavy—exhaustion, broken families, abandoned ministries. And when a pastor falls, the church feels the weight as well. Muchos de ellos cargan con innumerables responsabilidades: atender las necesidades de sus miembros, dirigir ministerios, dirigir reuniones, preparar predicaciones, etc… y en medio de todo eso, muchas veces olvidan cuidarse a sí mismos, a sus esposas y a sus hijos. El costo es alto: agotamiento, familias heridas, ministerios abandonados. Y cuando un pastor fracasa, también la iglesia sufre.

But in His faithfulness, God turned this need into a powerful vision: The Shabbat Christian Center. A place created to strengthen, restore, and equip pastors, leaders, and churches. A space where they can find rest, grow physically, emotionally, biblically, and spiritually, and where congregations can also come together for camps and gatherings that build their faith. Pero Dios, en su fidelidad, transformó esta necesidad en una visión poderosa: el Centro Cristiano Shabbat. Un lugar diseñado para capacitar, fortalecer y renovar a pastores, líderes e iglesias. Un espacio donde ellos puedan descansar, crecer física, emocional, bíblica y espiritualmente. También será un lugar donde las congregaciones podrán realizar campamentos y encuentros que edifiquen su fe.

The Shabbat Christian Center is more than just a project—it is a gift from God to His church. It is a reminder that in the midst of serving, the Lord calls us to pause, to recharge, and to be renewed in His presence. El Centro Cristiano Shabbat no es solo un proyecto, es un regalo de Dios para su iglesia. Es un recordatorio de que, en medio del servicio, el Señor también nos llama a detenernos, a recargar fuerzas y a ser renovados en Su presencia.

We praise God for the many ministry partners who have already joined this vision of God. Today, we invite you to join us in making this vision a reality. Together we can build a place that will bring life, hope, and strength to those who give everything for the Gospel. Your prayers and financial support will help turn the Shabbat Christian Center into a reality that will impact generations to come.

Join the vision—let us be God’s instruments of renewal for His church!
